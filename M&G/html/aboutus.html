About M&G

M&G is a digitalization of shopping experience for both shops and shoppers.M&G is a multi-vendor e-commerce platform that allows shop owners to make their virtual shop easily within hours using a M&G platform to change their classic way of selling. Shoppers accordingly will be able to navigate more products and have a more comfortable shopping experience. The platform is user-friendly that doesn't need technical knowledge or operational cost or digital marketing knowledge to use. It keeps shop owners focusing on selling and customer satisfaction. For shoppers, they will be able to reach an unlimited number of products with price comparison, a safe digitized experience using time-efficient and comfortable tools for shopping.

Who do you think M&G platform is serving?

M&G is serving two customer segments: The first one is shop owners who are not able to open their doors to customers as they used to and this affects their sales and their capabilities to survive through this situation, which might lead to their closure. Which will in turn lead to job loss for their daily wage labor and affect their families' livelihoods. So, M&G provides them with an easy tool that allows them to digitalize their stores within minutes and make them online and available for all their customers 24/7 at a low cost as compared to other channels of advertising (such as building their websites or pages on social media like Facebook and Instagram) which requires a level of technical or marketing experience. In addition, with no need for any coding prerequisites, they can guarantee their presence online. M&G is the new virtual experience for the offline shopping experience where almost every supplier is on the same platform and customers are there looking for their different needs. The second one is the shopper who has no time to go shopping after work due to the curfew timing, they are looking for some products within a certain budget, they want to experience the product before buying it and they want to enjoy this experience in a cost and time efficient way.
